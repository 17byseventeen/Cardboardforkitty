Register Your Team

Join us for Cardboard for Kitty 2026

Registration Options

To register a team for this year's event, you have two options. The choice is yours!

Monetary Registration

$20 per person

Pay the registration fee per team member to secure your spot.

In-Kind Donation

1 Medium-Large Box

Bring a box of donations instead of paying the registration fee.

Choose either:

  • Cat-related items for RedHead Strays
  • Items needed at First Steps Saint John

How to Register

1

Contact Us

Reach out to us via email or phone to express your interest in registering a team.

2

Choose Your Option

Decide whether you'll pay the $20 per person fee or bring an in-kind donation box.

3

Provide Team Details

Share your team name, number of participants, and which registration option you've chosen.

4

Confirm Your Spot

Once we confirm your registration, you're all set to build amazing cardboard cat homes!

Need Help with Donations?

If you need assistance gathering donations for your team registration, contact Grace. She can organize donation drives within her school to help you create a box of items for either RedHead Strays or First Steps Saint John.
