Sponsorship Opportunities

Support creativity, sustainability, and community impact

Join us in making a difference! Your sponsorship helps us support RedHead Strays and First Steps Saint John while promoting environmental responsibility and youth-led action through the 17 by Seventeen initiative.

Tier 1

Paw Partner

$500

A great entry-level way to support creativity, sustainability, and animal well-being.

Benefits include:

  • Recognition on Cardboard for Kitty social media (1 post or story)
  • Logo placement on event signage
  • Name listed on the Cardboard for Kitty website or event program
  • Thank-you mention during the event
Tier 2

Whisker Sponsor

$1,000

Expanded visibility and stronger connection to the community impact.

Benefits include:

  • All Paw Partner benefits
  • Logo featured on promotional materials and posters
  • Two social media mentions
  • Opportunity to include a small branded item or message at the event
  • Recognition in post-event recap content
Tier 3

Cat Home Champion

$1,500

Ideal for sponsors who want to be clearly associated with the event's mission.

Benefits include:

  • All Whisker Sponsor benefits
  • Prominent logo placement on event signage
  • Three social media features
  • Verbal recognition during opening and closing remarks
  • Option to have a branded judging category or award
Tier 4

Title Sponsor

$2,000

Top-tier partnership with maximum visibility and impact.

Benefits include:

  • All Cat Home Champion benefits
  • Featured as "Presented by [Sponsor Name]"
  • Logo included on all major event materials
  • Dedicated social media post highlighting the partnership
  • Opportunity to display a banner or table at the event
  • Special recognition as a key supporter of youth-led sustainability and 17 by Seventeen
In-Kind

In-Kind Donation

Value-Based

Support the event through goods, services, or materials instead of monetary contribution.

Benefits include:

  • Recognition based on estimated value of donation
  • Social media thank-you mention
  • Logo placement on event signage
  • Inclusion in post-event sponsor acknowledgements
